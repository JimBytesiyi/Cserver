William Maher is an American comedian, writer, producer, political commentator, actor, and television host.
He is known for the HBO political talk show Real Time with Bill Maher
and the similar late-night show called Politically Incorrect,
originally on Comedy Central and later on ABC.
In 2022, Maher started the podcast Club Random.
